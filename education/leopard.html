Leopard

Master of Stealth in the Wild

Leopards are silent and solitary predators with remarkable strength. They are capable of carrying heavy prey up trees to keep it safe from scavengers.

Quick Facts

Common Name: Leopard

Scientific Name: Panthera pardus

Type: Mammal

Diet: Carnivore

Lifespan: 12–17 years

Size: 1–1.9 m body length

Weight: 30–90 kg (66–198 lbs)

Found across Africa and parts of Asia, leopards are extremely adaptable and thrive in forests, savannas, and even mountains.

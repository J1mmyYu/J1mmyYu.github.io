Elephant

The Wise Giant of the Land

Elephants are the largest land mammals on Earth. They live in complex family groups and are known for their intelligence, memory, and ability to communicate with infrasound.

Quick Facts

Common Name: Elephant

Scientific Name: Loxodonta africana / Elephas maximus

Type: Mammal

Diet: Herbivore

Lifespan: 60–70 years

Height: Up to 3.3 m (10.8 ft)

Weight: 4,000–7,000 kg

Elephants shape their environment and are considered keystone species. Sadly, they face threats from poaching and habitat loss.

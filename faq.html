FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

📸 What is this website about?

This website showcases nature and wildlife photography while offering educational content about each animal. It combines beauty with knowledge for kids and nature lovers alike.

🌍 How are the animals categorized?

Animals are grouped into 3 folders: lands, seas, and skys, based on their natural habitats.

📖 What is the “Learn More” button for?

It links to a dedicated page with detailed facts, behavior, lifespan, diet, and other info suitable for educational use.

📷 Where do the images come from?

Images are licensed from public sources such as Pexels. Contributors like Magda Ehlers and Francesco Ungaro are featured in our About page.

👦 Who is this site for?

Kids, teachers, wildlife enthusiasts, and anyone curious about animals will find value here. Content is designed to be accessible yet informative.

📬 Can I send feedback or contribute?

Absolutely! Visit our About & Contact page to leave a message or connect with us.
